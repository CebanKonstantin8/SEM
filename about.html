Who We Are

The Service Management Society (SEM Society) at Copenhagen Business School is a student-led organization dedicated to fostering academic excellence and professional development in the field of service management.

Founded in 2015, our society brings together students, alumni, and industry professionals to create a vibrant community focused on learning, networking, and career opportunities in the service sector.

What We Do

Academic Support

We provide study resources, exam preparation materials, and tutoring services to help students excel in their coursework.

Career Meetups

Workshops and panels with HR professionals and recruiters to help students prepare for their careers.

Opportunities to work on real-world service management projects with partner organizations.
